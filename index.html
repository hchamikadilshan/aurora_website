About

Aurora 2024

Aurora - Conference on Computer Science, organized by the Computer Science Association at the University of Sri Jayewardenepura, stands as a beacon of innovation and inspiration. Hosting Aurora 2024 marks our sixth consecutive year of driving forward-thinking discussions on cutting-edge topics such as Generative AI, Large Language Models, Prompt Engineering, and Digital Entrepreneurship.

At Aurora, we believe in empowering the next generation of tech enthusiasts and entrepreneurs. That is why we have crafted a dynamic blend of competition and conference, tailored for both undergraduates and school students. Our aim is simple yet profound: to ignite imaginations, inspire visions of the future, and guide participants in charting their paths in the dynamic world of Computer Science and Technology.

Join us at Aurora 2024 to unlock your potential, engage with industry experts, and become a driving force in shaping the future of AI. Together, let's embark on a journey of innovation and transformation.

Aurora Competitions

School Competition

AI Quiz

School competition aims to inspire creativity and technological exploration among students through generative AI. Participants will attend a session which is done by industry experts covering basics of Generative AI, Large Language Models, and Prompt Engineering, followed by a quiz from the session. They will then compete in text summarization and question-answering challenges, applying Prompt Engineering practically. The winners will be choosen by combining both the points from the quize and the practical session. Join us to unlock your potential and contribute to shaping the future of AI!

More details can be found on the registration form

University Competition

AI Ideathon

University competition is crafted to inspire innovative startup ideas harnessing the potential of Generative AI. Participants are encouraged to propose novel applications, services, or products capable of making a significant impact on the future world through the utilization of generative AI. Round 1 involves submitting proposals, and in Round 2, the top 6 selected teams will implement their ideas using a generative AI API. To support their endeavors, we will provide up to $50 in credits per team to cover API costs. Join us in shaping the future with your creative ideas!
