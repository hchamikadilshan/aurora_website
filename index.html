School

AI Quiz

Join us for Aurora 2024's Competition for School Students, an exhilarating challenge designed to inspire innovation and ingenuity! Delve into the world of Generative AI and Large Language Models as you explore Prompt Engineering techniques and learn about the risks and rewards of working with advanced language models. Then, put your skills to the test in a thrilling competition where teams must summarize complex texts and answer questions with precision. Join us and unleash your creativity in this exciting competition!
